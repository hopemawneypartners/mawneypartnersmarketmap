Ronald de Gier is the Head of Portfolio Operations at Axar, which he joined 2023. Most recently, Ronald was a Senior Vice President at Houlihan Lokey, where he focused on M&A, capital markets and restructuring transactions in the firm's Global Technology group. Before Houlihan Lokey, Ronald worked at Pursuit Advisory, a boutique investment bank focused on the media and technology sectors. Prior to that, he worked at Credit Suisse in the firm's Technology, Media & Telecom and Leveraged Finance groups. Previously Ronald was a tax lawyer at Davis Polk and NautaDutilh, a Netherlands-based law firm. Ronald holds an LL.M from Erasmus University Rotterdam and an M.B.A. from Cornell University.

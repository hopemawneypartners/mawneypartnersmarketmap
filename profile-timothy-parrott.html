Timothy Parrott is the Co-Founder and President of Axar Capital Management L.P. ("Axar"), a private investment firm based in New York, NY. Axar specializes in investment in stressed and distressed corporate credit and hybrid capital investment solutions to corporations undergoing transition on behalf of institutional clients. Mr. Parrott leads the firm's business development and strategic initiatives, providing leadership for client relations and overall strategic direction. Before founding Axar, Mr. Parrott was a Partner and Co-Head of North American Investments for Mount Kellett Capital Management, a private investment organization with over $7 billion of assets under management. Mr. Parrott joined Mount Kellett at firm inception and worked there for over 6 years. Prior to joining Mount Kellett, Timothy worked at Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co. L.P. and The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc. Timothy graduated magna cum laude with a B.S. in Economics from Duke University.

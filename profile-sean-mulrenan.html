Sean Mulrenan is a Director on the Business Development & Investor Relations team at Axar, which he joined in 2020. Prior to Axar, Mr. Mulrenan was with New Amsterdam Group, where he advised clients on equity and debt transactions and asset sales across the oil & gas, industrial, and real estate sectors. Prior to New Amsterdam, he held various research and investing roles at Mount Kellett Capital, Spectrum Equity Investors and Interactive Brokers. He began his career as Corporate Legal Assistant at Cravath, Swaine and Moore. Mr. Mulrenan graduated with an M.B.A. from NYU Stern in Quantitative Finance with Distinction and with a B.A. in Political Economy magna cum laude from Williams College. He holds the Chartered Alternative Investment Analyst (CAIA) and Certified Management Accountant (CMA) designations. He has also received the CFA Institute Certificate in ESG Investing.

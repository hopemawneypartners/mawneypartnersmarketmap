Akash Kumar is a Senior Analyst on the investment team at Axar, which he joined in 2021. Prior to Axar, Mr. Kumar was a research analyst at Millennium Management, where he focused on equity investments across the consumer, industrial, and payments sectors. Prior to Millennium, he held various investing roles at Talpion Fund Management and HIG Capital. He began his career as a restructuring analyst at Houlihan Lokey. Mr. Kumar graduated summa cum laude with a B.S. in Economics from The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
